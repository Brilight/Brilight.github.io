# 👨🏻‍💻 Biography

I am a second-year PhD student in the <a href='http://www.aais.pku.edu.cn'>Academy for Advanced Interdisciplinary Studies </a> and <a href='https://ic.pku.edu.cn'>School of Integrated Cirtuits </a>, Peking University.
